Classes Schedule

Practice make one perfect. Attain continous focus via our classes where we encourage self practice. We have weekly group classes and individual classes by appointment.

Kids Yoga Classes - Weekly Sessions

Kids love fun and fun loves them. At Niramay Me, we teach yoga to kids in a way natural to them. Learning happens effortlessly.

Yoga for Adults - Weekly Sessions

Yoga is union with self. As adults, we are always running after completing tasks at hand. Yoga practice helps them slow down and connect to self and rejuvenate.

Group Meditation - Weekly Sessions

Meditation is about taking your attention from outer world to inner. Humans are social by default, the vibes generated together are more encouraging and effective.

Reiki Practice - Individual Sessions

Reiki is a spiritual healing art with its roots in Japanese origin. The word Reiki comes from the Japanese word (Rei) which means “Universal Life” and (Ki) which means “Energy”. Reiki is not affiliated with any particular religion or religious practice. It is not massage nor is it based on belief or suggestion. Read more

Hypnotherapy - Individual Sessions

Hypnotherapy is an alternative curative healing method that is used to create subconscious change in a patient in the form of new responses, thoughts, attitudes, behaviours or feelings. Read more.
